description: Compliance sign-off on any client-facing or regulatory document
argument-hint: <file path or description>
---

Use the `compliance-officer` agent to review the following for compliance sign-off: **$ARGUMENTS**

The reviewer must run through the standard checklist:
- Required disclosures present (fees, conflicts, risk warnings)
- Performance figures with proper benchmarks and time periods
- Forward-looking statements clearly labeled
- Jurisdiction-appropriate regulatory boilerplate
- For trades: client suitability, best execution, sanctions screening
- For filings: deadline compliance, completeness, sign-off chain

Output: APPROVED / APPROVED-WITH-CHANGES / REJECTED with specific issues identified.
